About Wool & Silk Art Studio

Welcome to the world of Svetlana Lilley, a master felting artist and passionate educator dedicated to preserving the ancient craft of working with wool and silk. Based in Neath, South Wales, Svetlana specializes in nuno felting, a delicate and artistic technique that fuses fabric and wool to create stunning, wearable art and intricate textile pieces.

With years of experience and an unwavering commitment to traditional felting methods, Svetlana brings history, heritage, and creativity together in her workshops. She works with locally sourced Welsh wool, celebrating the region's rich textile traditions while introducing modern innovations. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced fiber artist, her hands-on courses provide an inspiring, immersive experience, helping you master the art of felting in a relaxed and welcoming setting.

Join Svetlana and discover the magic of transforming raw wool into beautiful, handmade textiles - a skill as rewarding as it is timeless. Explore her workshops today and start your felting journey!
